Making the Transition

I am a regulatory attorney making the transition into full stack web development - and I love it!

I have found that that writing code draws upon my experience in the logic-based disciplines of philosophy and law in new and creative ways. I can apply my problem solving and deductive reasoning skills to the process of writing and debugging code, structuring data models and navigating unfamiliar environments.

The key to this transition has been the Galvanize Full Stack Program, where I am building a solid foundation in the programming languages and technology frameworks needed to develop web applications from front to back. In addition to working daily with Javascript, Node, PostgreSQL, Express and Angular, I have learned to evaluate the impact of design patterns, UI/UX concerns, data modeling, integration/deployment tools and agile methodologies on the development process. Group projects have taught me the importance of utilizing version control systems, pair programming and test-driven design to efficiently deliver successful products.

What is next ?

Now I am actively seeking a role as a junior developer in a collaborative environment that fosters growth and where the quality of the code I write matters. Ultimately, my goal is to be the developer who can efficiently deliver the right technology for the problem at hand, because I truly understand the “why” and “how” of the problem and the solution.
